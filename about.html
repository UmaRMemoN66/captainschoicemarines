Message From the MD

Welcome to Captain's Choice Marine Services, Apparently, a browse through our website will give you an overview of our business expanse. We believe, it showcases our expertise and experience in undertaking dry docking and major repairing of vessels.

The name Captain's Choice Marine Services has become synonymous with reliability, commitment, quality and prompt delivery in the marine industry in the India. The past 5 years have seen us building up the foundation for a successful and long-term partnerships with our clients. This has come through after a focussed work on every aspect of business, a feature which is crucial to our clients and customers.

Captain's Choice Marine Services was first established in 2019 as a small time marine company for ship repair and maintenance at Al Jadaf Dockyard. Today, we have grown and expanded to offer our clients a variety of services that span an entire range of ship repair and ship building operations. These include specialization in the manufacture of complete stern arrangement system, various type of winches, Kort nozzle and power pack.

In these past years, our employees have worked hard round the clock which has resulted in the goodwill and repute of the company. We have been accorded the title of ‘A real expert in the marine industry’, from the world around, thanks to their unstinted support. I heartily wish to express my sincere thanks to all our employees for their professionalism, dedication and hard work, at this juncture.

Our clients are our strength. Their trust and confidence on our services have seen us growing wider and stronger, all these years. I consider it my privilege to thank my clients and business partners for standing with us throughout this successful journey. We will strive to be worthy of their trust and fulfill our mission in the future.

Safety

Captain's Choice Marine Services considers Occupational Health & Safety (OHS) to be a core value in all its operations. Throughout our ship yard, OHS is promoted under the strap-line "Safety is our Highest Priority". Captain's Choice Marine Services has an OHS Team led by top-level management, supported by external consultants, which is dedicated to striving towards OHS excellence in the company.

Quality

Each of our yards have quality, safety and environment systems for Quality Management, Occupational Health and Safety and for Environmental Management.

Environment

We strive to perform business in a manner that will minimize threat to the environment as well as occupational hazards to the employees who live and work in that environment.

We primarily focus on meeting all applicable, federal, international and local legislative requirements.

Our aim includes improving upon our overall environmental performances and to avoid, reduce or control the creation, emission or discharge of any type of pollution or waste.

We consider it our responsibility to continuously improve our environmental performance by training our employees. We understand the importance of reducing the volume of waste products and maximization of reuse and recycling.
