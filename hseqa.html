Quality Policy

The main objective of the company is to identify the customers’ requirements and to provide quality and effective repairing and maintenance services for ships, marine vessels and industrial equipment. Our dedicated and committed staffs work towards the gratification of our clientele.

The top management promotes effective team work to strictly control and continually improve quality in accordance with the quality management system requirements.

Everyone in the Captain's Choice Marine Services is trained to understand that, his/ her everyday contribution in terms of duties, responsibilities, attentiveness, thorough communications and safety awareness are very essential in building a sound and effective quality environment.

We are committed towards the continual improvement of our capabilities and the Quality Management System we have implemented. We will function in the most cost effective method and will maintain our commitment to the safety and well being of our personnel.

OHS Policy

Captain's Choice Marine Services consider Occupational Health & Safety (OHS) to be a core value in all its operations. Throughout our ship yard, OHS is promoted under the strap-line "Safety is our Highest Priority".

Captain's Choice Marine Services has an OHS Team led by top-level management, supported by external consultants, which is dedicated to striving towards OHS excellence in the group.

Each of our yards have quality, safety and environment systems for Quality Management, for Occupational Health and Safety and for Environmental Management.

With a strong ground on our health & safety, we meet our customer requirements with reliability and commitment.

We also provide resources to meet the intended needs in a timely manner. We ensure compliance with relevant regulatory, legal and other requirements and take care to prevent injury & ill health of our employees, contractors and other persons who may be affected by our activities.

Captain's Choice Marine Services Shipyards is totally committed to maintain its OHS management system and it is mandatory that all employees accept this responsibility and comply with the specified system requirements. All employees should perform their assigned work in accordance with OHS company procedures and its OHS policies. This extends to all levels of Management and Supervision.

This is made known to all employees through the company’s induction program, individual distribution, and prominent display on the company’s notice boards. The message is also emphasized during all our internal training programs on a regular basis.
